About Kitty

I am a self-taught front-end web developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and growing experience with frameworks such as React. I’m passionate about building responsive, accessible, and user-focused websites, and I’m eager to apply my skills to real-world projects. In addition to seeking opportunities to gain industry experience, I’m also interested in freelance work where I can collaborate with clients to bring their ideas to life through clean, effective web solutions.
